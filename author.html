Abora Author

All work so far under the Abora project banner has been accomplished by David Jones. I can't claim any connection with the long history of the Xanadu project, but was aware in passing of Ted Nelsons work and the shadow of the Xanadu project. I was fascinated to read the much maligned Wired article, but finally started to get an inkling of the value of a Xanadu like system after reading Literary Machines followed by some brief investigations into the open-source release of Udanax-Gold.

If you are interested in contributing, or have any comments then don't hesitate to contact the author.
